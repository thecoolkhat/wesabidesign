HOW TO SUBMIT DESIGN REQUESTS

After subscribing, you are added to our project management tool, a platform that we have found to be both efficient and user-friendly over our years of experience. This environment serves as a seamless interaction space for both our team and our valued clients.

STEP 1

Step 1 stands as the sole obligatory phase within WeSabi Design process. The subsequent stages offer you the opportunity to provide comprehensive guidance to your designer, facilitating their ability to precisely capture your vision on the initial try. Rest assured, the option for limitless revisions remains available should the need arise.

Within Step 1, you will initiate the process by assigning a name to your design, determining the desired file size and dimensions, and furnishing a detailed description of your requirements. While the remaining fields in this step are voluntary, completing them can greatly aid your designer in comprehending the overall essence and stylistic direction you envision for your request.

Additionally, you possess the choice to input the intended audience for your design, furnish links to images or websites that serve as sources of inspiration, and provide insights into elements you wish to avoid. Concluding this section, you are able to designate the file format(s) required for your design. As a default, we will supply JPG, PNG, PDF, and Adobe source files.

STEP 2- Any assets on your design like images, etc

Moving on to Step 2, you'll be presented with an opportunity to explore the Unsplash and Getty Images databases. Unsplash is accessible to all clients without any charge, allowing you to search through a wide range of images. However, access to the Getty Images database requires an additional monthly fee of $25.

Some of our clients have their own stock images or other they want to include in their design which is also cool by us.

Should you prefer your designer to conduct the image search on your behalf, you can simply convey your requirements to them, and they will take care of the process. With this, let's proceed to Step 3!

STEP 3-UPLOAD YOUR ASSETS

Step 3 serves as the designated area for uploading any resources you wish to provide to your designer for your request. This can encompass items like logos, branding colors, a particular font, or even a hand-drawn sketch illustrating your vision. It's important to note that your drawing doesn't have to be perfect – our skilled designers are adept at transforming even a napkin doodle into exceptional graphic design.

As you establish a productive collaboration with your designer and acquire a solid grasp of what constitutes an effective request, you'll have the option to transition into sending your requests via email. This streamlined process enables you to seamlessly communicate your design needs to your designer.
